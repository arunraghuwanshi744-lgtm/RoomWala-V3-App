Privacy Policy

Welcome to RoomWala. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account details
  • Room listing information
  • Images uploaded by room owners

2. Authentication

RoomWala uses Firebase Authentication to securely manage user accounts and login services.

3. Room Data

Room owners can upload room details including images, rent, location, facilities, and descriptions. This information is used to display room listings to users.

4. Image Storage

Uploaded room images are stored securely using Cloudinary services and are used only for displaying room listings.

5. Data Security

We use security measures including Firebase security rules to protect user information and prevent unauthorized access.

6. Payments

Future payment services may be processed through secure payment providers. RoomWala does not store sensitive payment information.

7. User Rights

Users can request account information updates or deletion by contacting RoomWala support.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact RoomWala support.
